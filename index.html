About

Learn More About Us

We are an audio visual rental company based in the South of England. We rent gear to anyone in need of any equipment for any type of event.

We rent out professional sound gear to any event, we are very flexible with our hires:

  • We can rent gear out by itself.
  • We can rent gear out with a skilled and trained operator.
  • We can simply rent out one of our skilled and trained operators.

Our gear is of the top standard, we use industry standard gear from the top brands to ensure that it is reliable and good quality. Our professionals are trained and qualified in their field and will be able to resolve problems and run your show to an industry leading level.
